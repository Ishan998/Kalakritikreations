Our products are made using the traditional art of Chikankari, which began in the 16th century during the time of the Mughal emperors in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh also know as Awadh at that time. Known around the world for its detailed hand embroidery and floral patterns, Chikankari is done on soft, breathable fabrics that feel comfortable in both hot and cold weather. Every piece is handmade by skilled artists in Lucknow, and it takes several days to complete just one. This careful process makes each product special, showing the true beauty and tradition of Chikankari.
